## Writing Workflow

### Day Before (20 minutes)
1. Choose tomorrow's topic
2. Gather sources and links
3. Create outline
4. Collect images/data

### Writing Day (2-3 hours)
1. Write first draft (60-90 min)
2. Add images and formatting (15 min)
3. SEO optimization (15 min)
4. Proofread and edit (20 min)
5. Publish via admin panel (5 min)

### After Publishing (10 minutes)
1. Share on social media
2. Submit to Google Search Console
3. Engage with comments
4. Track analytics

## Time Management

### Realistic Publishing Schedule

**Starting Out (Month 1-2)**:

- Focus on quality
- Build foundation

**Growth Phase (Month 3-6)**:
- 4-5 posts per week
- Consistent schedule
- Build audience

- 5-7 posts per week
