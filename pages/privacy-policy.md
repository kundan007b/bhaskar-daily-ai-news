# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 1, 2025

## Introduction

Bhaskar Daily News ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Automatically Collected Information

**Cookies:**
- Language preference (English/Hindi) stored locally in your browser
- Session cookies for site functionality
- No personally identifiable information stored

**Analytics Data:**
- Page views and visit duration (via Google Analytics)
- Geographic location (country/region only)
- Device type and browser information
- Referral sources
- All data is anonymous and aggregated

### 2. Information You Provide

**Email Subscriptions:**
- Email address (when you subscribe to our newsletter)
- Subscription date
- Email preferences

**Contact Forms:**
- Name and email address
- Message content
- Submitted via Formspree (third-party service)

**News Submissions:**
- Information you provide when submitting news tips via GitHub Issues

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Deliver Content:** Remember your language preference
- **Send Newsletters:** Email you news updates (only if subscribed)
- **Improve Our Site:** Analyze traffic patterns and popular content
- **Respond to Inquiries:** Answer your questions and feedback
- **Display Relevant Ads:** Through Google AdSense (see below)

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site.

- **Data Collected:** Anonymous traffic statistics
- **Purpose:** Improve content and user experience
- **Opt-Out:** Use [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **More Info:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense.

- **Cookies:** Google uses cookies to serve relevant ads
- **Personalization:** Ads may be based on your browsing history
- **Opt-Out:** Visit [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
- **Third-Party:** Other vendors may also use cookies ([opt-out options](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/))
- **More Info:** [Google Advertising Privacy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### EmailJS

We use EmailJS to send subscription confirmation emails.

- **Data Shared:** Your email address
- **Purpose:** Send welcome emails when you subscribe
- **Storage:** Emails stored in GitHub Issues (backup)
- **Security:** Encrypted transmission
- **More Info:** [EmailJS Privacy](https://www.emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy/)

### Formspree

We use Formspree for our contact form.

- **Data Shared:** Name, email, message content
- **Purpose:** Process and forward contact form submissions
- **More Info:** [Formspree Privacy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/)

### GitHub

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Subscriber data is stored in GitHub Issues.

- **Data Shared:** Email addresses (for subscribers)
- **Purpose:** Subscriber management and newsletter distribution
- **Access:** Only site administrators
- **More Info:** [GitHub Privacy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)

## Data Retention

- **Cookies:** Stored until you clear them or they expire
- **Subscriber Emails:** Retained until you unsubscribe
- **Contact Form Data:** Retained for communication purposes
- **Analytics Data:** Retained according to Google Analytics policy (14-50 months)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of your personal data we hold

### Rectification  
Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

### Erasure
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")

### Unsubscribe
Stop receiving our newsletter at any time (unsubscribe link in every email)

### Opt-Out
- Disable cookies in your browser
- Use ad blockers
- Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
- Opt out of personalized advertising

### Data Portability
Request your data in a machine-readable format

**To exercise these rights,** email us at contact@kbhaskar.tech

## Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

### Necessary Cookies
- Language preference
- Session management
- Essential for site functionality

### Analytics Cookies
- Google Analytics
- Usage statistics
- Help us improve the site

### Advertising Cookies
- Google AdSense
- Personalized advertisements
- Can be disabled via browser settings

**Cookie Consent:** By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** HTTPS for all pages
- **Limited Access:** Only authorized personnel can access subscriber data
- **Third-Party Security:** We use reputable services (Google, GitHub, EmailJS)
- **No Payment Data:** We don't collect credit card or payment information

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using our site, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Third-Party Links

Our content may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Legal Compliance

- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **Information Technology Act, 2000** (India)
- **Google AdSense Program Policies**
