<section class="max-w-4xl mx-auto px-4 py-12 space-y-6">
  <p class="text-xs uppercase tracking-[0.3em] text-brand-100">Standards</p>
  <h1 class="text-3xl font-bold text-white">How we report, in English and Hindi.</h1>
  <p class="text-slate-200 text-sm">We follow strict verification and transparency guidelines to stay Google News compliant.</p>

  <div class="space-y-4 text-sm text-slate-200">
    <div class="rounded-2xl border border-white/10 bg-white/5 p-4 space-y-2">
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white">Sourcing</h2>
      <ul class="list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
        <li>Primary sources, on-record quotes, and public filings prioritized.</li>
        <li>Anonymous sources used only with editor approval and context.</li>
        <li>We cite and link to original documents wherever possible.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="rounded-2xl border border-white/10 bg-white/5 p-4 space-y-2">
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white">Fact-checking</h2>
      <ul class="list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
        <li>Dual-review for all breaking stories.</li>
        <li>Technical claims verified with subject-matter experts when needed.</li>
        <li>AI-generated assistance is reviewed by human editors.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="rounded-2xl border border-white/10 bg-white/5 p-4 space-y-2">
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white">Corrections</h2>
      <p>Corrections are issued within 24 hours of confirmation. Updates are timestamped in the article footer.</p>
      <p>Report issues: editorial@kbhaskar.tech (include URL and the correction requested).</p>
    </div>
    <div class="rounded-2xl border border-white/10 bg-white/5 p-4 space-y-2">
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white">Independence</h2>
      <ul class="list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
        <li>No undisclosed sponsored content.</li>
        <li>Advertising and editorial decisions are separated.</li>
        <li>Conflict of interest disclosures accompany relevant stories.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
